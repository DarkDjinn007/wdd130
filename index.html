Nigeria

Lagos is a major city located in the southwestern part of Nigeria, along the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. It lies approximately between latitude 6.5244° N and longitude 3.3792° E. As the most populous city in Nigeria and one of the fastest-growing cities in the world, Lagos serves as the country’s economic and cultural powerhouse. Originally a small Yoruba fishing village, it has grown into a sprawling urban hub that includes the mainland and a series of islands, such as Victoria Island and Lagos Island. The city is known for its bustling markets, lively music scene, heavy traffic, and dynamic mix of modern skyscrapers and traditional neighborhoods.
